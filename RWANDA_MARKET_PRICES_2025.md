## 📊 KEY PRICE INSIGHTS FOR CERKA

### Regional Price Variations
- **Kigali**: Highest prices, best availability
- **Musanze (North)**: Cheaper volcanic stone/aggregates, premium potatoes/carrots
- **Huye (South)**: Mid-range, good for coffee/tea
- **Kayonza/Rwamagana (East)**: Cheapest grains/rice, good for bulk
- **Rubavu/Karongi (West)**: Cheaper fish, good for tea/coffee

### Construction Price Surge (2025)
- Cement up ~20% due to Bugesera Airport and major projects
- Steel rebar nearly doubled (14,000 → 27,000 RWF per bar)
- Sand/gravel up 30-40% in Kigali
- Bricks truck load: 70,000 → 120,000 RWF

### Agriculture Seasonality
- **Beans**: Cheaper Jan-Mar (harvest), expensive Jul-Sep
- **Potatoes**: Cheaper Apr-Jun (Musanze harvest), expensive Oct-Dec
- **Tomatoes**: Cheapest Jun-Aug, expensive Nov-Jan
- **Coffee**: Harvest Apr-Jun, prices peak Oct-Dec
